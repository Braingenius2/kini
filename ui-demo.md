## Screen Story

### 1. Home / Quick Scan

- Upload screenshot
- Paste suspicious chat
- View recent alerts

### 2. Risk Result

- Risk score
- Short explanation of what triggered the warning
- Highlighted phrases or scam cues

### 3. Safe Response

- Polite boundary-setting reply
- Payment-safety reply template
- Suggestion to stop engagement if risk is high

### 4. Evidence Pack

- Timestamped conversation summary
- Extracted risky phrases
- Exportable proof bundle for record-keeping

### 5. Trend View

- Top scam phrases this week
- Repeat numbers or accounts
- Common tactics seen in the local market

## Demo Narrative

1. Seller receives a suspicious message.
2. Seller uploads the screenshot to Aya Shield.
3. Aya Shield flags a likely scam or abuse pattern.
4. Seller gets a safe reply and chooses whether to disengage.
5. Seller exports a record for protection and follow-up.
