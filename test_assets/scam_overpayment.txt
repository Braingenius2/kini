# Kini Test Case: High-Risk Scam (Fake Overpayment)

"Hi, I'm interested in the bag. I just sent the money via bank transfer, but oh no! I accidentally sent 150,000 Naira instead of 15,000. I am so sorry, I was in a hurry. Please REFUND the balance of 135,000 to my brother's account IMMEDIATELY. My bank is already pending the reversal. HURRY!"

---
**Why this is HIGH RISK:**
- **Fake Overpayment**: "accidentally sent", "reversal"
- **Urgent Tone**: "IMMEDIATELY", "HURRY"
- **Suspicious Payment**: "brother's account"
